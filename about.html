About

The Coffee Shop

is a cozy haven in the bustling city. Brie, Afia and Marilu, the passionate owners, opened this inviting spot to bring people together over high-quality coffee and delicious pastries. The warm wooden furnishings and soft lighting create a homey atmosphere where customers can relax and connect.
The menu features classic and unique coffee drinks, along with fresh-baked treats. Every month, The Coffee Shop hosts an "Open Mic Night," drawing local artists and fostering a sense of community.
Whether you're stopping by for a morning brew or enjoying an evening with soft jazz, The Coffee Shop is a cherished part of the neighborhood, where friendships are forged over a shared love of coffee.
